About Us

Rossy and Norma have 20 years of experience making beautiful and delicious cakes. She opened Rossy's Bakery & Coffee shop in the Lower East Side of Manhattan. Whether you live in New York City, or just visiting, this is a great place to go and indulge with great treats.

Our Product

We specialize in Dominican style cakes for any occasion. Please stop by our bakery today for a cafecito, empanada, or a delicious Dominican homestyle cooked meal.

Delivery & Pickup Options

At our business, we understand the importance of choice and flexibility when it comes to receiving your products. That's why we offer a range of delivery and pickup options to suit your needs. You can opt for our convenient doorstep delivery service, which ensures your items are safely transported to your preferred address within the promised timeframe. Alternatively, if you prefer a more hands-on approach, we also provide a hassle-free pickup option at our designated locations, allowing you to collect your purchases at your convenience. We believe in putting our customers first and providing them with the freedom to choose the delivery method that best suits their lifestyle.
